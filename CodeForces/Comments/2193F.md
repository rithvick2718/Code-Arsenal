# 2193F

This is a DP problem. The driver cannot move backward horizontally, so he must complete all deliveries vertically at the current horizontal level before moving forward. Notice that the driver has two options when finishing a horizontal level: he can end the deliveries either at the highest point or at the lowest point while still delivering optimally.

Therefore, the dynamic programming approach is used to compute the minimum time required to finish each horizontal level while ending either at the top or at the bottom, based on the results from the previous horizontal level.
